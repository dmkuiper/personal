Poker On the Go!

Poker On the Go! is related to developing a simple poker app using Android Studio. When using the app, you enter bet amount and declare a winner of the hand. When someone runs out of chips, they are no longer able to make bets. Once there is only one person with chips remaining, a winner screen will appear.
